What is noun ?
Noun is a Naming word person,things,place.
Four types of noun
  1. Proper noun - specific names
    2. Ex: Kumar , Gokul , neem tree etc.
  2. common noun - name in general
    3. Ex: Man , Women , bird , tree etc.
  3. collective noun - names of group
    4. Ex: team , people , audience etc.
  4. Meterial noun - names of meterial
    5. Ex: gold , silver , iron etc.
Abstract noun
